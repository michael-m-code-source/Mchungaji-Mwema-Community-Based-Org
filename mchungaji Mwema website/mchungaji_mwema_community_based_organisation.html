About Us

Mission: To provide quality early childhood education, food, and care to children in the Kuwinda slums through love, learning, and community support.

Vision: A thriving future for every child through inclusive, community-led education.

Our Story: Founded by local caregivers and supported by international donors (primarily from the U.S.), we support vulnerable children under 10 with education, meals, and play in a safe environment.

Our Programs

  • Early Childhood Education: Creative, fun, and literacy-rich curriculum.
  • Daily Feeding Program: Nutritious meals for children who often go without at home.
  • Play & Social Activities: Safe space for games, arts, and team-building.
  • Parental Support & Community Involvement
